Questions:

1. What is the difference between CHAR and VARCHAR?

2. What is the limit of CHAR?

3. What time is it, in seconds since the beginning of time?

4. How many times faster is O(log n) than O(n) for n = 18721693

Answers:

  1. The difference between CHAR and VARCHAR is that CHAR is faster and is limited at 255 bytes. VARCHAR is more flexible and is limited to 65535 bytes. Bytes can be wasted when using CHAR. VARCHAR uses only the amount of bytes needed plus one(1) stop byte.
  2. The limit of CHAR is 255 bytes.
  3. The beginning of time: 1970.01.01 00:00:00 UTC
  4. Approximately: 1523629800
